LIVERPOOL 1-1 CRYSTAL PALACE

DARWIN NUNEZ GETS SENT OFF FOR A HEADBUTT ON HIS HOME DEBUT AS HOSTS ARE HELD, Aug 16, 2022

Liverpool have recorded two points in their opening two Premier League matches of the 2022-23 campaign after playing out a 1-1 draw against Crystal Palace. Wilfried Zaha's first-half goal was cancelled out by Luis Diaz in the second, but the match will be best remembered for Darwin Nunez headbutting Joachim Andersen on his home debut which resulted in a straight red card.

JUVENTUS 3-0 SASSUOLO

ANGEL DI MARIA SCORES ON HIS SERIE A DEBUT AS JUVENTUS BEAT SASSUOLO IN STYLE, Aug 16, 2022

Angel Di Maria had a debut to remember for Juventus as he opened the scoring for his new club on his debut in their Serie A opener. It was a straightforward night for Juve in Turin as Dusan Vlahovic scored a further two goals for the hosts. He made it 2-0 just before half-time from the penalty spot before he wrapped up the victory in the 51st minute with an easy goal set up by Di Maria.
